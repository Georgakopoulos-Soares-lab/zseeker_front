Privacy Policy


ZSeeker is committed to your privacy. We want to make it clear that our service does not collect or store any personal data. Our system is designed to accept job submissions, process them on the server in real-time, and return the results directly to you without saving any data.

1. Data Collection and Storage

ZSeeker does not collect any personal data. We do not maintain a database or any persistent storage for user information. Every job submitted is processed immediately and the results are returned without retaining any details.

2. How Data is Processed

When you submit a job, it is handled by our server and the corresponding results are sent back to you instantly. No logs or user data are stored beyond the scope of that transaction.

3. Data Access and Third-Party Transfers

Since no personal data is collected or stored, there is no access granted to any administrators or third parties, and no data is transferred externally.

4. Cookies and Third-Party Resources

While ZSeeker may use cookies strictly for essential website functionality, these cookies do not collect any personal data. Additionally, third-party resources (such as jQuery, FontAwesome, Bootstrap 5 Datatables, and ChartJS) operate under their own data policies. We do not share any user information with them.

5. Your Rights

Because ZSeeker does not collect, store, or retain any personal data, there are no data privacy rights applicable regarding personal information. Every transaction is ephemeral, ensuring complete privacy.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please visit our About page.
